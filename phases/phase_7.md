# Phase 7 – Interview Intelligence

## Goal

Generate a complete **AI Interview Kit** for every shortlisted application, helping students prepare from one place.

---

# Phase 7.1 – Interview Dashboard

### Tasks

* View upcoming interviews
* Select an application
* Track interview status

**Output**

* Interview workspace.

---

# Phase 7.2 – Company Intelligence

### Tasks

Generate:

* Company overview
* Products & services
* Tech stack
* Recent news (optional)
* Role expectations
* Company culture

**Output**

* Company Summary.

---

# Phase 7.3 – Interview Questions

### Generate

* HR Questions
* Technical Questions
* Behavioral Questions
* Role-specific Questions
* Company-specific Questions

**Output**

* Personalized interview question bank.

---

# Phase 7.4 – STAR Answer Generator

### Tasks

Generate STAR-based answers using the student's:

* Resume
* Projects
* Experience
* Career Profile

Examples:

* Tell me about yourself
* Biggest challenge
* Leadership experience
* Conflict resolution

**Output**

* Personalized STAR responses.

---

# Phase 7.5 – Interview Notes

### Generate

* Key topics to revise
* Important skills
* Resume highlights
* Questions to ask the interviewer
* Last-minute checklist

**Output**

* One-page Interview Notes.

---

# Phase 7.6 – AI Suggestions

### Tasks

Provide advice such as:

* Highlight Project X
* Revise Docker concepts
* Prepare System Design
* Mention Internship Y
* Practice behavioral questions

**Output**

* Personalized preparation recommendations.

---

---

# Final Output

By the end of Phase 7, every application can generate an **AI Interview Kit** containing:

* ✅ Company Summary
* ✅ Personalized Interview Questions
* ✅ STAR-based Answers
* ✅ Interview Notes
* ✅ AI Preparation Tips

The student can open any application from the Kanban board and instantly prepare for that specific interview, making this a seamless continuation of the application workflow rather than a separate tool.
